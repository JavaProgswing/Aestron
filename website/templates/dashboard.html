PLAYER CONTROL

Data and consent

The final product exposes these controls to every linked user.

Explicit Riot opt-in

Authentication happens on Riot's domain. Aestron stores the linked Riot identity and permitted match history, never the password or transient OAuth tokens.

Visibility

Only opted-in players are analyzed. The Discord bot defaults to private responses for personal coaching and does not support pre-match scouting.

Unlink and delete

The unlink command removes the Riot identity and cached match references associated with the Discord user.
