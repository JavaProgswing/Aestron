02 / SECURE LINKING

Your account.
Your choice.

Riot hosts authentication. Aestron never asks for your Riot password and does not persist OAuth tokens.

01

Start in Discord

Run /linkaccount to receive a private, signed link that expires after ten minutes.

02

Authorize on Riot

Riot confirms the account and returns the permitted identity to Aestron's HTTPS callback.

03

Review or unlink

Use /vstats and /valcoach, or remove the link with /unlinkaccount.

Opt in clearly.
